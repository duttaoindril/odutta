Projects

  • Waffle is a location-based social app with which the user can share photos of his or her surroundings, and will pop-up on the newsfeed of others in the area. Waffle ties the photos to that location for a period of 24 hours and displays in a real-time photo feed to all of your friends present in the area.

    Check it out!

  • CoderAIcer is a webapp;the user can write AI to race a car through a track, dealing with turns, such as walls, the road, and other cars. This gets competitive when people put their AI online in a match, so that multiple user-programmed AIs race against each other, and the best AI wins, the best programmer wins.

    Check it out!

  • Trouvéis a webapp to share the location of anything, and find anything. Let's say you're hosting a garage sale or you found some great parking. Simply add that location to the master map on Trouvé, and anybody can access it to find that parking/garage sale, and you can use it to find anything else too!

    Check it out!

  • Seekr is a webapp that pulls in 100,000 jobs from the Dice API, each with a physical location, and displays those jobs on a map;the jobs can be searched for by location and skill, and can be filtered for you top skills. It uses the Dice job skills to generate analytics for the user in a beautiful graphical format.

    Check it out!
